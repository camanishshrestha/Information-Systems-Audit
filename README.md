# Project : Information Systems (IS) Audit & IT GAP Assessment — Banking & Financial Sector

## 📌 Overview
This project covers IS Audit and IT GAP Assessment engagements across commercial banks and corporate groups in Nepal (2021–2025). The focus was on evaluating IT governance, information security, and system controls using COBIT, ISO/IEC 27001:2022, ISACA standards, and NRB IT guidelines.

## 🎯 Objective
- Assess security, reliability, and governance of IT systems  
- Identify control gaps and cybersecurity risks  
- Ensure compliance with regulatory and global standards  
- Strengthen IT control environment and risk management  

## 🛠️ Key Work Performed
- Conducted risk-based IS audit planning and scope definition  
- Reviewed IT governance, policies, and risk management (COBIT)  
- Assessed information security controls (ISMS, encryption, endpoint, physical security)  
- Performed access control audits (RBAC, SoD, MFA, privileged access)  
- Evaluated network security, firewall rules, VPNs, and vulnerability management  
- Audited Core Banking Systems (Finacle, Pumori, T24) controls and audit trails  
- Reviewed BCP/DRP, backup processes, and disaster recovery readiness  
- Assessed change management and patch management processes  
- Led ISO/IEC 27001:2022 GAP Assessment (93 Annex A controls) across 9 companies  
- Prepared IS audit reports with risk ratings and remediation plans  

## 📊 Key Outcomes
- IS audits conducted for major commercial banks  
- Full ISO 27001:2022 GAP Assessment completed for 9 companies  
- 93 Annex A controls evaluated with maturity and risk mapping  
- Improved IT governance, security posture, and compliance readiness
